NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
1
Add weight ONLY when every prescribed rep is completed with clean form across all sets.
2
If reps drop more than 2 between sets — you rested too short OR the weight is too heavy. Adjust both.
3
Heavy compounds (5–8 reps): 3 min rest. Unilateral/dynamic: 90 sec. Accessories: 60–90 sec.
4
Nordic Curls: Start at 3 sets Day 1, 2 sets Day 3. Do not increase until Week 4–5.
5
Jump Squats: 30–50% of squat weight only. This is power training, not a max lift.
